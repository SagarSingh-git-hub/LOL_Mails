Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Data Collection

We are strict about data minimization. LOL Mails does not collect personal data. We do not require you to register, provide a real email address, or create a password. We do not store your IP address alongside your activity.

2. Email Retention

All emails received by our temporary addresses are stored in volatile memory. They are retained for a maximum of 10-60 minutes (depending on server load) and then permanently deleted. Once deleted, they cannot be recovered by anyone, including us.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser Local Storage solely to maintain your temporary email session and save your theme preferences. We do not use cookies for cross-site tracking, advertising profiling, or identifying you personally.

4. Analytics

We use anonymous, privacy-focused analytics to understand general site usage patterns (e.g., "how many users visited today"). No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected or transmitted to third parties.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Since we do not collect personal information in the first place, we have nothing to share.

6. Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
